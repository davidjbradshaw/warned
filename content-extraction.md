### English (`/en/warned.html`)

**Tagline:** We Are Retired Not ExpireD!

**Main Introduction:**

The city accentuates the feeling of isolation: meeting places are disappearing, neighborhood relations are weakening and ageism is insidiously taking hold in the organization of society. Older people are particularly vulnerable to loneliness and social isolation - especially in cities - and this can have serious consequences for their health. Studies suggest that loneliness could increase the risk of premature death by a quarter or more and be as harmful as smoking 15 cigarettes a day. It is the responsibility of all of us to encourage intergenerational connection and to lift the elderly out of loneliness.

A new phenomenon is here: more time is available to today's generation of seniors. Life has lengthened to such an extent that we face twenty, thirty, even forty years of life beyond retirement. What to do with this gift? What projects can we envisage that prove suitable, fruitful, realistic and inventive? How can a positive "aging culture" emerge that both provides seniors with better living conditions and also gives meaning to what they experience? We have to be intelligent and creative in order to tackle "sustainable aging" head-on. This is where WARNED comes in.

**About WARNED:**

WARNED - We Are Retired Not ExpireD - is a non-profit which dedicates itself to the creation of unique initiatives, events and activities that activate the senior community. We aim to revolutionize the world of seniors by actively fighting ageism and highlighting the positive role played by retirees within society. We help seniors - GOLDIES - to pursue more active and fulfilling social lives, and have fun! We promote intergenerational relationships and social cohesion. We add value through the sharing and transfer of professional skills. We encourage seniors to celebrate their age with confidence and pride.

**Our Pillars:**

These three philosophical pillars form the bedrock of all WARNED's initiatives:

1. **Social Connection**
   - Thé Dansant emphasizes social connection between GOLDIES and their community via friendship and personal relationships. By providing a supportive, safe and fun environment, we encourage intergenerational mixing, peer-to-peer relationships and active participation.

2. **Emotional Wellbeing**
   - At the heart of our initiative is the belief that all GOLDIES should have the space to develop self confidence, to boost self esteem, and to improve mood which all carry forward into their daily lives. We make it a priority to offer emotional support to grow.

3. **Professional Power**
   - Initiatives celebrate the deep professional experience that all our GOLDIES have to offer, no matter their field of expertise. We recognize the enduring vitality of a senior workforce by creating projects that aid professional connection and knowledge sharing.

**Our Values:**

- **Inclusivity:** We are open-minded and respectful of difference. Everyone is welcome equally, regardless of their gender, sexuality, race, religion, politics, marital status or profession.

- **Participation:** Self expression and participation is key. There are no spectators! Everyone is encouraged to participate fully in all activities and freely express their individuality.

- **Autonomy:** We want everyone to feel autonomous - especially physically, socially and emotionally. We encourage all our participants to feel free to move and express themselves.

**Our Benefits:**

Our pillars and values bring long-lasting benefits to our GOLDIE community. These benefits aim to positively transform their everyday lives:

- Personal Relationships: Finding and developing meaningful companionships, love, joy and friendship
- Spontaneity and Curiosity: Seizing the day and having active curiosity in every new experience
- Intellectual Engagement: Facing activities which challenges us to think, reflect and react with courage and confidence
- Intimacy: Finding emotional intimacy between partners/friends and the (re-)awakening of one's sensual, sexual and spiritual selves
- Generosity: Making time for each other and sharing one's skills, expertise, empathy and time freely with those around us
- Co-Creation: Collaborating together in order to allow a sense of pride and civic responsibility in our community

**About GOLDIES:**

GOLDIES - the community of seniors 65+ - are the beating heart of WARNED. Regardless of marital status, gender, sexuality, race, politics, religion, economic background or profession, all GOLDIES are welcome to participate in the unique events, activities and initiatives that WARNED creates.

At WARNED, we aim to release the FLAMINGOLD in all our GOLDIES. Perfectly embodying the spirit of the GOLDIE, the pink flamingo represents the mastery of emotions, cooperation, openness and love, while being charming, graceful and serene. It traditionally symbolizes one's social nature and the desire to create community bonds in which we can all thrive.

Voornamelijk gevestigd in Brussel en Amsterdam, stroomt ons collectief over van energie om onze gemeenschap van GOLDIES te betrekken en de derde leeftijd met vertrouwen en trots te vieren. Alles is geïnspireerd en ontworpen om het FLAMINGOLD op te wekken die in ons allemaal, jong of oud, sluimert. In de geest van samenwerking en co-creatie zijn we consequent op zoek naar uitbreiding van onze drijvende kracht - daarmee bedoelen we JOU!

Of het nu gaat om het doneren van je kostbare tijd of je unieke vaardigheden, we kunnen van je leren en dit culturele en sociale project samen laten groeien.

**Sluit je aan bij Team Thé Dansant:**
Kom het team versterken dat onze extravagante amusementsmiddag, Thé Dansant, produceert! Thé Dansant is altijd op zoek naar nieuwe vrijwilligers om de GOLDIES te helpen voorbereiden, verwelkomen, bedienen, begeleiden en vergezellen tijdens dit fantastische evenement. We zijn er trots op een luxueuze, attente en unieke Engelse Afternoon Tea-ervaring te bieden.

Neem contact met ons op door het formulier onderaan de pagina in te vullen.

**Thé au Jardin:**
Kom het team versterken in de organisatie, de receptie en het beheer van Thé au Jardin. We zijn altijd op zoek naar nieuwe vrijwilligers om onze Thé au Jardin-activiteiten voor te bereiden of om onze GOLDIES te verwelkomen en te begeleiden. We zijn ook actief op zoek naar mensen die deel willen uitmaken van het onderhoudsteam van Ukkel Freespace (herstellingen, tuinieren, klaarzetten). We hebben een breed takenpakket en bieden veel verschillende verantwoordelijkheden om van Thé au Jardin een bevredigende en veilige omgeving te maken voor onze GOLDIES.

**Artist or Workshop Instructor:**
Creativiteit bloeit bij ons! Ben je een artiest? We horen graag van degenen die in cabaret, muziek en uitvoerende kunst werken om bij te dragen aan onze "Thee" -evenementen. Wil je een workshop leiden? We zijn altijd blij om te spreken met gekwalificeerde professionals op het gebied van wellness, therapie, spiritualiteit, uitvoerende kunst, creatieve bezigheden en sport om ons wekelijkse programma te verbeteren.
